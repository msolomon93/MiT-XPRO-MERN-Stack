Fenway Park

Americas Oldest Ballpark!

Since 1912, Fenway Park has been the home of the Boston Red Sox. Located in the heart of Boston near Kenmore Square, the stadium has undergone major renovations during its lifetime with the first major remodel coming in 1934 and the latest being completed earlier this year. It currently holds the award for Oldest active ballpark in the USA and, among other quirks, has a host of unique elements that seperate it from the other stadiums around the country such as "The Triangle", Pesky's Pole, and the Green Monster overlooking left field.

Fun Facts about Fenway
  • Holds up to 40,000 spectators
  • Registered as a historic landmark
  • Sold out every game from May 15, 2003 - April 11, 2013
  • Formerly hosted games for the New England Patriots
